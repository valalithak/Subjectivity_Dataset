‘Dangal’ box-office collection Day 16: Aamir Khan film becomes Bollywood’s highest grosser
 Aamir Khan''s ' Dangal ', which also stars Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sanya Malhotra, Sakshi Tanwar, Zaira Wasim and Suhani Bhatnagar in key roles, has beaten 'PK' and Salman Khan's 'Bajrangi Bhaijaan' and 'Sultan' to become the highest-grossing Hindi film of all time.  'Dangal' has surpassed the lifetime collection of 'PK' (net.) Rs 340.8 crore today, having earned Rs 341.96 crore at the domestic box office till Day 16.
