Deepika-Ranveer wedding and reception: Best memes
Deepika Padukone and Ranveer Singh made our year beautiful with their gorgeous, fun-filled wedding. All their pictures are pure love in a frame.The couple had an intimate wedding celebration at Lake Como, Italy. The celebrations in Italy comprised of sangeet, mehendi, a few Konkani rituals and two ceremonies - a Konkani wedding and Anand Karaj.Post that, the couple hosted three reception parties for their close friends and families. The first one was held in Bengaluru for Deepika's side of relatives on November 21.This was followed by a party thrown by Ranveer's sister Ritika for the newlyweds on November 24. The next function was another reception in Mumbai for Ranveer's relatives on November 28.The celebrations finally came to an end yesterday i.e. December 1 with a grand star-studded bash for the film fraternity and celebrities from other walks of life.Well, so many functions means more chances for people to come up with hilarious memes and reactions.
