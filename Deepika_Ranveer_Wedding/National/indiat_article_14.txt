Sanjay Leela Bhansali, who got Deepika and Ranveer together, at Mumbai reception
Wedding festivities are still not over for one of Bollywood's most popular couples. If the pictures from Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone's dreamy wedding at Lake Como made many hearts melt, the newlyweds are busy giving couple goals with each of their public appearances post wedding. After grabbing eyeballs with their two wedding receptions, the couple is now letting their hair down with their Bollywood friends at Mumbai reception.When almost everyone is awaiting the arrival of biggies like Shah Rukh Khan, Aamir Khan and Salman Khan, all eyes are on Sanjay Leela Bhansali at Deepika and Ranveer's Mumbai reception. And why not? It was on the sets of his 2013 film Goliyon Ki Rasleela Ram Leela that love blossomed between Ranveer and Deepika. While Ranveer has admitted that he knew Deepika was the one, six months into dating, the 32-year-old actress took her own time to take their relationship to the next level. The real and reel-life romance that began in 2013 solidified with 2015 Bajirao Mastani.
