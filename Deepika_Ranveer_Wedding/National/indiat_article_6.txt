Ranbir skips Mumbai reception of ex-girlfriend Deepika and Ranveer
It has been a month of non-stop wedding fesitivities for Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone. The couple kicked off the celebrations with a Nandi puja, haldi ceremony, and then headed to Lake Como, Italy for mehendi, sangeet, and two wedding functions on November 14 and 15.The party didn't stop there, as the couple returned to India on November 18, and threw three receptions, on November 21, 28 and December 1. The last one was for the Bollywood fraternity.Most of B-Town's who-who were present at the bash, including Shah Rukh Khan, Anushka Sharma, Katrina Kaif, Varun Dhawan, Amitabh Bachchan and family, Sidharth Malhotra, Jacqueline Fernandez, Rekha, Karisma Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Saif Ali Khan, Ayushmann Khurrana and Tahira Kashyap.There were a precious few who were not invited and didn't make it to the bash. Salman Khan was busy with his upcoming film Bharat.
