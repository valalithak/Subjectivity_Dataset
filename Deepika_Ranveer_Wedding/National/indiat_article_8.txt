Salman Khan did not attend Deepika-Ranveer Mumbai reception. Here's why
With the grand star-studded reception in Mumbai, Ranveer Singh and Deepika Padukone's wedding and post-wedding festivities finally came to a end.Most of Bollywood's who-who were present at the bash, including Shah Rukh Khan, Anushka Sharma, Katrina Kaif, Varun Dhawan, Amitabh Bachchan and family, Sidharth Malhotra, Jacqueline Fernandez, Rekha, Karisma Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor Khan, Saif Ali Khan, Ayushmann Khurrana and Tahira Kashyap.However, there were some who were conspicuous by their absence. While Sushant Singh Rajput and Kangana Ranaut were not invited to the reception, Salman Khan wasn't able to attend the bash.Our sources told us Salman said that he would try; but could not make it. He was caught up with work on upcoming film Bharat. His co-stars Katrina Kaif and Disha Patani attended the bash instead.Ranveer and Deepika tied the knot with two ceremonies in scenic Lake Como, Italy on November 14 and 15. They returned to India on November 18, and hosted a bash for the Padukone's extended family and friends on the 21st.
