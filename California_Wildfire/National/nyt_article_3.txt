The list of those unaccounted for after the Camp Fire in California has grown to 700 people, according to estimates from the Butte County Sheriff’s Office on Monday.  If you are on the list or have located someone who is, please contact the Butte County Sheriff’s Office. It has set up a call center that operates daily from 8 a.m. until 8 p.m. The call center numbers are: 530-538-6570  530-538-7544  530-538-7671 If you fled the fire, but you’re not on the list, please visit the American Red Cross’s website to register yourself as safe and well.  As we continue to report on this tragedy and try to understand what has happened to the missing, we welcome news from you.
