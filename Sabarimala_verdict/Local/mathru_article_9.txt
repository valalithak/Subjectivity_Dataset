Thiruvananthapuram: Oposition leader Ramesh Chennithala said the Devaswom Board has to file a a review petition in connection with the SC verdict on Sabarimala. Ramesh Chennithala made this statement following the protest by K Sudhakaran against the verdict.
"The practical difficulties and social impact of the SC verdict need to be considered. The Supreme Court must consider those issues," Ramesh Chennithala said in his statement.
The verdict was the result of the foul play by Left government which didn’t take a specific stand on the issue. He alleged that the double stand taken by the government has adversely affected the case.
