K J Yesudas contributes Rs 10 lakh for flood relief
Singer K J Yesudas is the latest celeb to rise to the occasion to help Kerala.

To help rebuild the state affected by floods, he donated a huge sum to Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan yesterday. Along with his wife Prabha, the singer gave Rs 10 lakh to the donation fund of the State. 

Earlier, P C George MLA had said that many singers, incluiding Yesudas didn't help the state during floods. Meanwhile, the CM had said Yesudas had given him a call, aand assured all help required.
