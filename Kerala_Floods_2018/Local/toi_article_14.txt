Veteran actress Jayabharati contributes Rs 10 lakh to Kerala flood relief
Jayabharati, who was the dream girl of a generation of Malayali movie audience, steps in to help the State that rewarded her with many memorable characters and achievements. 

The actress, who has been away from the limelight for a while, was recently at the State Secretariat in Thiruvananthapuram to meet Chief Minister Pinarayi Vijayan. She handed over a draft of Rs 10 lakh to the CM, to help the State as part of the flood relief operations currently under way. It's at 10 am today that she met Pinarayi Vijayan. 

She joins the league of many cine celebs who met the CM in person and handed over their contributions to the noble cause.
