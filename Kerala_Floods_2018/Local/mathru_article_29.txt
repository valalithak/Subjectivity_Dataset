Kochi: Ernakulam District Collector has appealed to the public that 50,000 food kits are needed to be distributed to the flood-affected people in the district.
He asked the public to bring the kits to the Rajiv Gandhi stadium in Kadavanthra.
The kits will be air dropped to the flood-affected people in various parts of the district.


Ernakulam is one of the worst-affected districts in the state.
