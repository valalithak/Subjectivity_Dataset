Kerala floods: Dulquer donates his remuneration from an inaugural function to CM's relief funds
Actor Dulquer Salmaan, the hearthrob of Malayalis recently donated the remuneration he received from an inaugural function at Karunagappally, Kollam to Chief Minister's Kerala Flood Relief Funds . Hundreds had gathered to catch a glimpse of the actor.

Addressing the crowd, Dulquer revealed that the owners of the jewelry had postponed the inauguration a few times due to the floods. "Happy to see the crowd. Thank You all,"he said.
